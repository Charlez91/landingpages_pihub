PiHub FAQs

What is PiHub?

PiHub is a Nigerian based company whose primary goal is to encourage the global adoption of Pi digital currency as an acceptable medium of exchange. PiHub is a multi-functional third-party utility.

Must i become Pioneer before i can use PiHub?

Yes

Difference between Pi and PiHub?

Pi is a digital currency used as payment on the PiHub applications.

When is the Pi Mainnet?

According to the Core Team of Pi Network, mainnet is at the forth quarter of the year 2021

Can i still mine Pi after the Pi Mainnet?

The decision to mine after Pi Mainnet will be determined by the Pi Network Core team.

About Us

PiHub is a third party Utility App developed by Pioneers. Its goal is to create Utility Apps where Pi can be used for the exchange of goods and services. PiHub is a multi-functional Utility App.
